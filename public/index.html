Expo

React Native

Djmote helps you figure out what music to play at your events. Create an event, invite people to RSVP, and let them link their Spotify or Apple Music and pick playlists they'd want to hear. Djmote compares everyone's picks and shows the songs that overlap the most, so you know what the crowd's into. Guests can also request specific songs and like others they want played, giving you a live sense of what people want to hear.

I helped lead in completely rebuilding the app's frontend to transition from a flawed and buggy web view frontend wrapper to using React Native and Expo. I also took our founder's new features and requirements and brought them to life, giving me a hand in almost every single major part of Djmote's experience.

The issue Djmote solves for parties is a very real one, and has garnered tons of interest from party organizers who wish to play the best music for their guests.

Eyeofophidia.net is a tool that catalogues all recorded matches for a hero based trading card game called Flesh and Blood TCG. Users can search for matches by player's names, event names, dates, and by hero matchup.

All match and event data is catalogued on the site manually by either me or trusted community members given admin access to the site.

Eyeofophidia.net has had over 4100 unique visitors from over 59 different countries around the world. Players of the card game have found the site tremendously useful especially right after large tournaments.

As of July 2024, this project has become under the wing of the game's best video production company, Savage Feats.
